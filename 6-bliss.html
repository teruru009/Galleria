Bliss

K.L. Abecia

2022

"Bliss" captures a timeless moment of intimate connection and shared happiness between two individuals. The centerpiece of the artwork portrays a woman gently holding the arm of a man, their silhouettes seamlessly merging into a harmonious dance of love and companionship. A moment, a slice of life, and a distant memory of the artist.

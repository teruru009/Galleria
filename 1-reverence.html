Reverence

K.L. Abecia

2023

In the mesmerizing tableau of 'Reverence the artist captures the delicate dance between reverence and the uncontainable brilliance of light. The canvas breathes with an ethereal glow, where hues of gold, amber, and celestial blues converge in a celestial symphony. At the heart of this luminous spectacle stands a lone figure, a man whose silhouette is etched against the radiant backdrop. The man's clinging arms tell a silent story of yearning and awe as he attempts to encapsulate the unruly radiance but it's too much for him. As he try to hold it longer, the more he realize that it's too much for him. The light remains unyielding, cascading and weaving through the air with an otherworldly grace.

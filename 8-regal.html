Regal

K.L. Abecia

2022

"Regal" captures the essence of timeless dignity in a single, contemplative moment. The painting unveils a scene of a man seated with an air of quiet confidence, his figure a testament to the grace found in simplicity. The subject, adorned in a casual ensemble, dons his favorite khaki beanie, an emblem of personal comfort and familiarity. The warm tones of the khaki beanie harmonize with the subdued palette of the surroundings, creating a sense of cohesion and tranquility. The play of light and shadow adds depth to the composition, casting a gentle glow upon the man's features, which are marked by a combination of wisdom and introspection.
